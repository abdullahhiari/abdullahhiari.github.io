XT Cart

The project aims to develop a cost-effective and portable device tailored for Jordanian households, addressing the specific need to transfer, lift, and position heavy objects like refillable 20-liter water bottles and reusable gas tanks. The device must adhere to a set of essential features and constraints, including compatibility with locally prevalent water bottles and gas tanks, maximum durability, lightweight design, minimal storage space requirements, low maintenance costs, user-friendly operation, safety, and manufacturability within the existing XT-Tools capabilities. The primary goal is to create a practical solution that is not only efficient in handling heavy items commonly found in Jordanian homes but is also affordable, user-friendly, and meets stringent durability and safety standards.

The project involves designing a versatile cart with the capability to carry both water bottles and gas tanks. The cart is equipped with a lifting mechanism that allows it to elevate these items to a height of approximately 1 meter.

After reaching the desired height, the cart possesses a flipping mechanism, enabling it to invert its load. This functionality serves a crucial purpose in distinguishing between water bottles and gas tanks. Once inverted, a sensor system is employed to identify the contents of the load.

If the sensor detects a water bottle, the cart intelligently places it on a designated cooler. This innovative solution streamlines the process of transporting and sorting water bottles and gas tanks efficiently, demonstrating a practical and automated approach to handling different items in a specific environment.
